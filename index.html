Lionel Messi

Biography

"Lionel Andrés Messi, also known as Leo Messi, is an Argentine professional footballer..." Lionel Andrés Messi was born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina. From a young age, he displayed exceptional football talent but was diagnosed with growth hormone deficiency at 10. In 2000, FC Barcelona offered to pay for his treatment, and he moved to Spain, joining La Masia, the club’s youth academy. Messi made his senior debut for Barcelona in 2004 at just 17. Over 17 years, he won 10 La Liga titles, 4 UEFA Champions Leagues, and 6 European Golden Shoes, becoming Barcelona’s all-time top scorer (672 goals). In 2012, he set a record by scoring 91 goals in a calendar year.
